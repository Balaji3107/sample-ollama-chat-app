ABC Bank Terms of Service and Loan FAQ (2024)

Section 1: Savings Account Interest Rates

Standard savings accounts (Tier 1) accrue interest at a rate of 1.15% APY. Premium accounts (Tier 2, minimum balance $10,000) accrue interest at 2.50% APY. Interest is compounded monthly on the 1st of every month.

Section 2: Personal Loan Terms

The maximum personal loan amount is $50,000. The minimum loan repayment period is 12 months, and the maximum is 60 months. The standard APR for a 24-month loan is 8.5%. Early repayment is permitted without penalty.
